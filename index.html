About

Svart is a chess engine written in Rust. It began as a fun project in February 2023 but quickly grew into a competetive engine. According to the CCRL, Svart is one of the top 100 strongest chess engines available with an elo rating above 3000, making it superhuman in strength. Svart employs a classical alpha-beta search with a modest implementation of an NNUE for evaluation.
