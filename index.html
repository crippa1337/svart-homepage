About

Svart is a chess engine written in Rust. It began as a fun project in February 2023 but quickly grew into a competetive engine. Svart employs a classical alpha-beta search with a modest implementation of an NNUE for evaluation.

Svart is written by me, Cristopher. I'm an 18 year old student from Sweden who enjoys programming and chess. But I did not write it alone, I have had help from many people along the way, especially from the Engine Programming discord server. A special thanks to the contributors of Svart.

Strength

According to the CCRL, Svart is one of the top 100 strongest chess engines available with an elo rating above 3000, making it superhuman in strength. It has repeatedly placed first in it's division in the CCRL Amateur Series.
